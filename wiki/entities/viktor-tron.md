# Viktor Trón

**What it is:** Author and lead architect of the Swarm network.

## Notes
Viktor Trón authored *The Book of Swarm*, the comprehensive design compendium for [[swarm]], and is its lead architect. The work lays out Swarm's layered architecture — [[libp2p]] underlay, [[kademlia]]-based DISC chunk overlay, the data-access layer (files, manifests, feeds, access control), and the economic incentive system ([[swap-protocol]], [[postage-stamps]], the [[redistribution-game]]).

Within [[decentralized-storage-networks]], Trón is the individual figurehead for Swarm, analogous to the organizational roles of [[protocol-labs]] ([[filecoin]]) and [[storj-labs]] ([[storj]]).
