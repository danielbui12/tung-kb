# Storj Labs

**What it is:** Storj Labs, Inc. — the company that builds and operates the Storj network.

## Notes
Storj Labs authored the [[storj]] v3 framework and whitepaper and builds the reference implementation of the network: the [[uplink]] client tooling, [[storage-node]] software, and the trusted-but-blind [[satellite]]s (the company operates approved Satellites, though anyone may run one). The design deliberately avoids blockchain on the hot path in favor of coordination avoidance to reach exabyte scale, with payments settled monthly in the ERC-20 STORJ token.

Within [[decentralized-storage-networks]], Storj Labs is the organizational counterpart to [[protocol-labs]] ([[filecoin]]/[[ipfs]]) and [[viktor-tron]]'s [[swarm]].
