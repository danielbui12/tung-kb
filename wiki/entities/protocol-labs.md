# Protocol Labs

**What it is:** The organization behind the IPFS/IPLD/libp2p content-addressing stack and the Filecoin network.

## Notes
Protocol Labs builds the foundational technologies of the content-addressed decentralized web: [[ipfs]] (content-addressing and transport), [[ipld]] (the Merkle-DAG data model and codecs), [[libp2p]] (modular P2P networking), [[multiformats]] (self-describing value formats), and [[filecoin]] (the storage-incentive blockchain and persistence layer). These projects share the [[content-addressing]] / [[merkle-dag]] backbone and interoperate by design.

Within [[decentralized-storage-networks]], Protocol Labs anchors the IPFS+Filecoin ecosystem, contrasting with [[storj-labs]] ([[storj]]) and [[viktor-tron]]'s [[swarm]].
