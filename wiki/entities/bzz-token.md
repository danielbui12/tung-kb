# BZZ Token

**What it is:** The native token of [[swarm]], used to pay for storage and bandwidth and settled on [[gnosis-chain]].

## Notes
BZZ funds two separate accounting systems: **storage** via [[postage-stamps]] (prepaid, decaying batches bought on-chain) and **bandwidth** via the [[swap-protocol]] (off-chain signed cheques cashed through a chequebook contract). Storers earn BZZ through the [[redistribution-game]]. Postage and redistribution smart contracts live on [[gnosis-chain]]. Compare [[fil-token]] (Filecoin, also a consensus stake) and [[storj-token]] (Storj, payment only).
